Shahi tukda

Description

Shahi tukda is a light sweet dish prepared with bread slices and milk, it is very well known in India and Pakistan.

It is extremely quick and simple to make with few ingredients. It will sure make a special place in your heart once you try it.

Ingredients

  • Bread slices - 4-5 (Or according to your need)
  • Milk - full cream, 2-3 cups per Bread slice
  • Ghee (Clarified butter)
  • Cardamom powder - 1 teaspoon, or to taste
  • Sugar - 1.5 tablespoon per cup of milk (or to taste)
  • Saffron (Optional)
  • Nuts - Almond, Cashew nut etc.

Steps

  1. Pour the milk in a frying pan, boil it on medium-high heat and keep stirring it until it gets thick and half of the milk remains.
  2. Add sugar, Cardamom power, nuts, saffron, and cook it for 4-5 minutes.
  3. Turn down the heat to low or turn it off while you prepare the bread slices.
  4. Fry the bread slices in Ghee (clarified butter) on medium to high heat until Golden brown.
  5. Once fried, put the bread slices in the prepared milk and let it soak.
  6. Serve them in a plate and top it with more nuts and dry fruits of your choice.
  7. You can also top it with sugar syrup or honey.
  8. Enjoy!

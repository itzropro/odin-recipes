Pancake

Description

Pancakes are life savers when you are craving something delicious and decide to prepare it by yourself at home.

This quick and easy pancake recipe puts the store-bought stuff to shame.

Ingredients

  • Flour - 1 cup
  • Sugar - 2 tablespoons
  • Baking powder - 2 teaspoons
  • Salt - 1/2 teaspoons, or to taste
  • Milk - 1 cup
  • Vegetable oil - 2 tablespoons
  • Egg - 1 large, beaten

Steps

  1. Prepare all the ingredients
  2. Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl.
  3. Make a well in the center of the flour mixture.
  4. Pour in the milk in the center, and pour in the beaten egg (You can also directly crack the egg in the mixture).
  5. Add vegetable oil, and mix them all together until a smooth batter forms.
  6. Heat a lightly oiled frying pan over medium-high heat.
  7. Pour 1/4 cup batter per pancake on the frying pan, and cook until bubbles form and the edges are dry, then flip it and cook until it gets light brown on the other side.
  8. Repeat the process with remaining batter.
  9. Top it with some butter, honey, and Enjoy!

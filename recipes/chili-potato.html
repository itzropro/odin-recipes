Chili potato

Description

One of the dishes which is worth putting efforts in is got to be the Chili potato, this sweet and savory potato dish seems to satisfy every one of your taste buds.

Try it once and this will be in your favorites list forever.

Ingredients

  • Potato - 1 or 2 large
  • Tomatoes - 3 or 4 Large
  • Capsicum - 1 Large
  • Garlic - 2 to 3 cloves
  • Ginger - 3 to 4 centimeter piece
  • Onion - 1/4 slice of a medium sized onion
  • Cumin seeds - 1 teaspoon
  • Chili power - 1/2 teaspoon or to taste
  • Vinegar - 1/3 to 1/2 cup
  • Soy sauce - 1 tablespoon
  • Sugar - 1/2 to 1 tablespoon
  • Salt - to taste

Steps

Prepare potatoes

  1. Peel and wash the potatoes, and chop them in long, 2 centimeter thick sticks.
  2. Boil them for 5 minutes on the high heat.
  3. Take the potato sticks out and strain the water.
  4. Deep fry them in a fryer or a frying pan until golden brown.
  5. Keep them in a separate container after cooked.

Prepare the gravy

  1. Cut the tomatoes in two halves
  2. Take water in a container and add tomatoes, piece of ginger, garlic cloves, onion and 3 to 4 pinches of salt in the container.
  3. Boil them until the tomatoes get soft and their skin starts to peel off.
  4. After boiled separate them from the water, and keep the water in a container, and cool them down, then add boiled tomatoes, ginger, garlic, onion, and 1 cup of the water that we kept in the container in a grinder and make a smooth paste.
  5. Now add 1 or 2 table spoons of vegetable oil in a frying pan, add 1 teaspoon of cumin seeds and cook them for 10-15 seconds, then add chopped capsicum and cook them for 5 to 8 minutes or until the they get soft, After that pour the tomato paste in the pan and cook it for 1 minute.
  6. Then add 1/4 to 1/2 cup (5 to 6 tablespoons) of vinegar and mix it well.
  7. Add 1/2 to 1 teaspoon salt, 1 to 2 tablespoons of sugar, and 1/2 teaspoon of chili powder, or keep them according to taste.
  8. Now add 1 tablespoon soy sauce, and boil it for 5 to 8 minutes on medium-high heat.

Complete the dish

  1. Once gravy is ready, add fried potatoes in the gravy and cook it for another 2-3 minutes.
  2. Serve warm and Enjoy!
